who are we?


Swan Cleaners has been an establishment since 2000. We specialize in all kinds of cleaning, from machine dry-cleaning to hand washing, and both tailoring and alterations. We pride ourselves in promoting nontoxic cleaning and respect the environment by recycling our hangers. Our Yelp reviews are the perfect way to experience our consistent and passionate customer service if you haven't experienced it yourself. We truly believe that clothes deserve the same respect no matter how old or dirty they are. Check us out on Facebook and Yelp! We look forward to doing business with you and treating your clothes to the best of our ability.
